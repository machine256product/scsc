About me:

I try my hardest to practice my skills and build my video game between my hours at work.

  • I am obssesed with lighting and surface texture. I create PBR maps from my own photos. I often stare at surfaces inside and outside, imagining how to author them digitally. I often stare at how light moves through a space. I have worked on a GLSL pipeline for the Godot compositor that emulates impressionistic raytraced PBR lighting effeciently on older hardware, (my radeon rx 570 does fine, at least), (150fps with SSR-like reflections that can reflect around corners or behind you.).

  • I am obsessed with the fusion of [beautiful/minimal] sounds with [aggressive/maximal] sounds. I am obsessed with integrating guitars into hardcore electronic and experimental music. I am obsessed with music and art as a comfort to those who feel they are uniquely irredeemable or badly different, as a way to show them I see a lot of what they see, their potential, and them.

  • I am obsessed with a specific melancholy. Sometimes forgotten people, sometimes forgotten places. Always imagery of streetlights in rain. Always rose petals on paving stones, nestled in the grass of a chiming church on a hill. Always the dust seen from the stained glass in the rafters. Always a lavender dream-snake coiled patiently behind a door, in a hallway of endless doors. This is a personal inspiration.
